Why folks nest here

Comfy on purpose.

Every mismatched chair and thrifted lamp was chosen so you'd stay longer than you planned to.

Our story

Two mugs & a dream.

Junie bakes. Marcus pours. In 2023 they turned a former bike shop on N High into the café they always wished existed — one where the chairs don't match, the playlist is somebody's mixtape, and the barista knows your order by week two.

Homebird is for Clintonville, Beechwold, Old North, and anyone who believes a neighborhood needs a third place that isn't anyone's living room but feels like everyone's.
